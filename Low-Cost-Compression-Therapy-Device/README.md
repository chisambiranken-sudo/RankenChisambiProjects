# Low-Cost Compression Therapy Device

## Overview

The Low-Cost Compression Therapy Device is a biomedical engineering project developed to improve access to compression therapy in low-resource settings. The device was designed as an affordable and portable alternative to expensive commercial compression therapy systems used for the management of venous disorders, lymphedema, and related circulatory conditions.

## Problem Statement

Many healthcare facilities and patients in Malawi have limited access to compression therapy devices due to high costs and dependence on imported equipment. This creates challenges in the management of patients requiring regular compression treatment.

## Project Objective

To design and develop a low-cost, portable, and easy-to-use compression therapy device that can provide controlled compression while remaining affordable and accessible for healthcare facilities and communities in resource-constrained environments.
