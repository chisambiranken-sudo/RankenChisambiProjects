# Smart Neo Baby Incubator

## Overview

The Smart Neo Baby Incubator is a low-cost neonatal care solution developed to help maintain safe temperatures for newborn babies, particularly in resource-constrained healthcare settings. The project was designed to address challenges associated with neonatal hypothermia, which remains a significant contributor to newborn morbidity and mortality.

## Problem Statement

Many healthcare facilities in low-resource settings face challenges in accessing and maintaining commercial neonatal incubators due to high costs, limited availability, and maintenance requirements. This can compromise the quality of care provided to newborns who require controlled thermal environments.

## Project Objective

To design and develop an affordable neonatal incubator capable of providing temperature regulation and monitoring while using locally available materials and components.

## System Operation

The incubator continuously monitors the internal temperature using sensors. When the temperature falls below the desired range, the heating system is activated. When the temperature rises above the set threshold, cooling fans are activated to maintain a safe environment for the newborn.
